Summer Go Camp

June 26 - September 1, 2023

Enjoy full or half day Go activities with your friends. All ages and levels welcome!

NYIG Camps are designed so that students can experience fun Go activities in a setting outside of class and learn from hands-on practicing. Some activities include tournament preparation, practice games, reviews, and team Go.
