Tournament Overview

The US Go Ranking Competition is a 5-round tournament for players to promote to a higher rank. This tournament supports promotion from 24k to 1d. Players that promote above 10k must participate in an AGA-rated tournament for the rank to become official. Contestants are separated into divisions and play against players of similar rank. A Ranking certificate can optionally be purchased. Visit this page for a brief introduction to Go ranks.

 Eligibility Participants must have a valid AGA rating, previous ranking certificate, or register as 24 kyu upon completion of the NYIG beginner course. Players must be 1k or under.

Divisions

The table below shows each division, group, and range of ranks. Participants are guaranteed to be paired within the same division, but may be paired with a player from a different group.

Division Group Range
Division A: Single-digit kyu A1 4k — 1k
A2 9k — 5k
Division B: Double-digit kyu B1 10k — 14k
B2 19k — 15k
B3 24k — 20k

Promotion Requirements

The table below shows promotion requirements in each division. The player's rank change as soon as enough games are accumulated for promotion.

Example: A 9k player in the A2 Group that wins 3 or 4 games will be promoted up to 7k.

Example: A 20 kyu player enters the B3 Group and wins 1 game. The player is immediately promoted to 19 kyu and plays the remaining rounds in the B2 Group.

Division Promotion
1k to 1d 3 consecutive wins or 5 accumulated wins
Division A: Single-digit kyu 1 win promotes up 1 level
3 wins promotes up 2 levels
5 wins promotes up 3 levels
Division B: Double-digit kyu Each win promotes up 1 level to a maximum of 4 levels
