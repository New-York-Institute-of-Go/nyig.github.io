Go Camp

Enjoy full or half day Go activities with your friends.

All ages and levels welcome!

Register

NYIG Camps are designed so that students can experience fun Go activities in a setting outside of class and learn from hands-on practicing. Some activities include tournament preparation, practice games, reviews, and team Go.

COVID-19 on-site limitation: To ensure the safety of our students and staff, the total number of students registered in the on-site camp is limited to 10. We will strictly adhere to NYC health guidelines. We also ask that all on-site students wear a mask at all times.
